qx: the probability that someone aged exactly x will die before reaching age x+1
px: 1-qx the probability that someone aged exactly x will survive to age x+1
lx: the number of people who survive to age x
dx: the number of deaths between ages x and x+1
mx: central rate of mortality. ~= average force of mortality, averaged over the year of age
Lx: the number of person-years lived between exact ages x and x+1
Tx: the number of person-years lived after exact age x
ex: life expectancy at age x
